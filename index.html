About

Three years of supporting principal-level operators — and I still get excited by a well-built system.

I started as an Executive Partner at Athena, one of the most rigorous EA training programs in the industry, where I was selected as 1 of 3 hand-picked EAs for their VIP client track — the accelerated program that prepared me for my first principal-level engagement.

From there I spent 2.5 years with Kiva Dickinson, the Founder and Managing Partner of Selva Ventures, a consumer-focused venture capital firm in Los Angeles. I ran his inbox, owned his calendar end-to-end, maintained an 8,000+ contact CRM, screened 200+ MBA intern applicants, and operated as a single point of accountability across work and life — including logistics involving his family and household.

Today I support the Founder and CMO of a Dutch global direct-to-consumer brand on a part-time basis, where I've built and now operate a custom partnership and referral platform end-to-end, and handle brand protection work including DMCA and IP enforcement across Shopify, Meta, and TikTok. I'm looking for one additional principal to support.
